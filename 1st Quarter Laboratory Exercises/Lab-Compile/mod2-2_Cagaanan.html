Brand New Animal (BNA)

Brand New ANimal or BNA for short is an Anime based on a world full of Humanoid Animals and Humas trying to co-exist. The huge problem in this Anime is the distrust between the two because of how Animals are seen as predators. Although they have created a city to seperate the two, problems still occurs. We watch the main character, Michiru, Find its way to cure her own unknown disease, finding other problems along the way and looking into the lives of the Anthropomorphic Animals.

Top 5 Anime Characters

  1. Michiru Kagemori
  2. Shirou Ogami
  3. Marie Itami
  4. Nazuna Hiwatashi
  5. Yuji Tachiki
